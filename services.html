Dental Veneers

Dental Bridges / Crown

A crown caps a damaged tooth to restore its strength and appearance. A bridge fills the gap left by a missing tooth by anchoring to adjacent teeth — both are custom-made for a natural look.

A strong, minimally invasive tooth replacement option using fibre-reinforced composite material. It bonds directly to adjacent teeth without crowns — a great alternative to conventional bridges.

Fluorosis Treatment
